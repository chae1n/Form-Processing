An HTML/CSS/JS Shipping Information Example by Julia Johnson

The following is a demonstration of a form that can be filled out and when data is entered, the data can be seen on the page.

Any form fields that have names with an underscore between significant words, such as First_Name will be split into separate words, for example First Name upon submission.

Upon submission, the shipping info is displayed and title case is applied to the Shipping Info.
